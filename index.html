Paper published in JCAM (Elsevier)
Projected exponential Runge–Kutta methods for preserving dissipative properties of perturbed constrained Hamiltonian systems.

In this paper, we develop geometric integrators for dissipatively perturbed Hamiltonian systems with constraints. The proposed integrators are able to preserve the phase space of the system and conformal symplecticness. Higher order geometric integraors for the system can be obtained by composition.
